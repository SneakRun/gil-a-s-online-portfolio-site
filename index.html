I am a process-led, multidisciplinary creative technologist, working across

creative code, digital design, 3D, and Game Engine work. The intersections of

process,play and the symbiosis of humans with technology fascinate me,

& I aim to foster collaboration between tools, technologies, and people

.

I studied BA Fine Art with Creative Computing at Central Saint Martins UAL, and

my work spans multimedia installations, interactive games, and experimental virtual worlds

that work to challenge traditional design structures.
